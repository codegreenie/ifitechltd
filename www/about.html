Company Overview

IFITECH AND ASSOCIATE L.T.D is a leading player in the real estate and construction industry in Nigeria with over twenty years on hand experience in that sector. It has its offices strategically located in three (3) states of the federation with the corporate head office situated at Ibeju Lekki, Lagos State. The company was formally incorporated under the Companies and Allied Matters Act 1990 in 2008 as IFITECH AND ASSOCIATES LTD , a wholly owned Nigerian company with a global perspective and adopting global industry best practices in the discharge of its services to its valued clients with the backing of cutting edge technology. The company’s business model is hinged on the need to provide excellent and affordable properties in real estate market necessitated by the high demand for low cost housing in Nigeria. The company also has a track record of accomplishments and proven ability on other aspects of property development such as; BUILDING, ARCHITECTURAL, CIVIL ENGINEERING, LAND SURVEY and PROPERTY CONSULTANCY.

IFITECH AND ASSOCIATES LTD is the promoter and developer of two thriving estates in the fastest growing real estate hub in sub-Saharan Africa: the Ibeju Lekki axis, popularly called the new Lagos: these are West Point Gardens LTD (Phase 1-9) and Solid Base Gardens LTD.(Phase 1-6) Making a total of fifteen estates scattered all over the three locations in the country. The Two registered estates with its phases are located in areas that have substantial upward appreciation potential with obvious good return on investment..

The company has subsidiaries, which are;

  • IFITECH UNIQUE INDUSTRIES LTD; an industry established mainly for the production of various construction/building materials.
  • Excellent Property Magazine Limited: This handles the mass communication, public relations and media arm of the company. With its wide circulation it disseminates and Supplies real estate information for investors and practitioners across the country.

The company’s foremost strategy to be a leader in the real estate industry resides in its ability to locate and acquire economically sound, high yield investment and continually providing enhanced services, relationship and sustainable profitability. In addition to property development, IFITECH AND ASSOCIATES LTD also actively pursue the acquisition of fair value properties by leveraging our extensive knowledge on land survey for families and communities as well as our proprietary, analytical and concessionary prowess that is guaranteed to generate significant financial return on investment.

We are also able to control cost and provide quality execution of our projects by keeping operating expenses as low as possible. We achieve this by engaging the company’s construction team to negotiate and ensure strict conformance on quality and procedures.
